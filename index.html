Welcome! I am Bingnan Xiao, a Ph.D. candidate (Excellence Doctoral Program) in the School of Information Science and Engineering at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xin Wang (IEEE Fellow).

Currently, I am supported by the China Scholarship Council (CSC) to conduct research at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), collaborating with Prof. Tony Quek (IEEE Fellow, Fellow of the Academy of Engineering Singapore).

My main research interests lie in federated optimization, agentic AI, and signal processing.
